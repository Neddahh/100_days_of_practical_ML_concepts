# 100 Days of Practical ML Concepts

Full disclosure: I have used AI (CLAUDE SPECIFICALLY) as a mentor, teacher, my research buddy and coding partner. The lecture notes for each day are hand_written (with little to no AI). The study guides in some cases were partially suggested by the AI as well however. Every material shared has been personally vetted by me for relevance.
